# ChainPulse Design System

This document directs design agents on how ChainPulse should look and feel.

## Aesthetic Goals
- **Vibe:** Sleek, modern, and "appeasing to the eyes" dark mode.
- **Audience:** Crypto power users, developers, and institutional traders.

## Color Palette
- **Backgrounds:** Soft, sophisticated dark grays (to reduce eye strain) rather than absolute black.
- **Primary Accent:** Soft emerald green (Solana vibe, signaling successful transactions/positive growth).
- **Secondary Accent:** Delicate, muted purple for highlights and AI insights.

## Typography & Shape
- **Font:** Inter or Roboto - clean, readable, sans-serif.
- **Corners:** Slightly rounded (approx 8px) to soften the analytical UI.

## Key Screens
1. **Landing Page:** 
   - Needs to feature an AI mascot gracefully integrated into the hero section.
   - High-converting, showcasing the "Dune + AI" value proposition.
2. **Dashboard:**
   - Sidebar navigation.
   - Real-time data widgets and Dune-style visualizations.
   - A dedicated AI Insights Panel surfacing plain-language actionable signals.
